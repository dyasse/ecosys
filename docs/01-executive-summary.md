# 1. Executive Summary

## What This System Is

This is a complete, self-improving AI automation ecosystem built to sell your digital crochet, knitting, and planner products online. It is designed for a solo creator who wants to build a real business — not a spam operation — using ethical marketing, genuine content, and smart automation.

## The Core Promise

You create products. The system handles:
- Getting them found (SEO + social)
- Turning visitors into email subscribers (lead magnets)
- Nurturing subscribers into buyers (email sequences)
- Turning buyers into repeat customers (segmentation + offers)
- Improving itself every week (analytics + AI review)

## Business Model at a Glance

```
TRAFFIC SOURCES
├── Pinterest (free, SEO-driven, visual)
├── Instagram Reels / TikTok (free, video)
├── Google / Blog (free, long-term SEO)
└── Facebook Groups (free, community)
          │
          ▼
LANDING PAGE (your website)
          │
          ▼
LEAD MAGNET (free PDF / template)
    → Email collected with clear consent
          │
          ▼
EMAIL SEQUENCE (5 automated emails)
    → Deliver value → Build trust → Present offer
          │
          ▼
PRODUCT SALE
    → Etsy / Gumroad / Lemon Squeezy / Your own checkout
          │
          ▼
REPEAT CUSTOMER
    → Tagged in email list → Gets future offers
```

## Revenue Streams (Phased)

| Phase | Revenue Source | Effort |
|---|---|---|
| MVP (Month 1) | One product on Etsy/Gumroad | Low |
| Month 2-3 | 3-5 products + email list selling | Medium |
| Month 4-6 | Blog SEO traffic + affiliate links | Medium |
| Month 6+ | Your own checkout, bundles, membership | High |

## Why This Stack

| Need | Tool | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Website | Next.js + Vercel | Free hosting, fast, SEO-friendly |
| Database | Supabase | Free tier, real database, scales |
| Email | Brevo | Free up to 300/day, GDPR compliant |
| Automation | n8n (self-hosted) or Make.com | Visual, beginner-friendly |
| Analytics | GA4 + Search Console | Free, industry standard |
| AI | Claude + ChatGPT | Content, code, strategy |
| Products | Gumroad → Lemon Squeezy | Zero monthly fee to start |

## What Makes This Different

1. **Legal by design** — opt-in only, unsubscribe always present
2. **AI-assisted, not AI-replaced** — you stay in control
3. **Content-first growth** — no paid ads required to start
4. **Compounding value** — every blog post, pin, and email grows over time
5. **One system, infinite products** — add new products to the same machine
