## What NOT to Do (Common Beginner Mistakes)

**Don't do this:**
- Launch on every platform at once — you'll do all of them badly
- Buy social media followers — worthless, harms your account
- Send emails before setting up SPF/DKIM — emails go to spam
- Price your products too low — $2.99 patterns signal low quality
- Wait until everything is perfect to launch — ship now, improve later
- Ignore analytics — you'll keep making the same mistakes
- Create a second product before your first one is working
- Pay for ads before your funnel converts organically
- Post to Instagram without a strategy — post with purpose or don't post

**Do this instead:**
- Master ONE platform before adding another
- Focus on organic growth for 6 months
- Let data tell you what to improve
- Ship fast, iterate based on real feedback
- Build your email list above everything else — it's your only owned asset

## Your Owned Assets vs Rented Platforms

| Asset | Owned | Risk |
|---|---|---|
| Email list | YES — you own it | If Brevo changes, you export and migrate |
| Your website | YES — your code | Zero |
| Blog content | YES | Zero |
| Pinterest followers | NO — rented | Platform could ban you or change algorithm |
| Instagram followers | NO — rented | Platform could change reach overnight |
| TikTok followers | NO — rented | Platform ban risk (US regulatory) |
| Etsy shop | NO — rented | Etsy can shut your shop |

**The lesson:** Build your email list above everything. It is the only audience you truly own.
