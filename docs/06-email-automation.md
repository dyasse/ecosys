**Preview Text:** `This took me months to figure out — I'll save you the trouble.`

**Body:**

```
Hi [First Name],

I hope you've had a chance to look through your free guide!

Today I want to share something I wish someone had told me early on, 
because it would have saved me weeks of frustration.

— THE GAUGE PROBLEM —

Most beginners skip the gauge swatch. 

I get it — swatching feels like a waste of time when you're excited 
to start your project.

But here's what happens when you skip it:

Your finished blanket is 3 inches too small.
Your hat slides down over your face.
Your bag is so loose it won't hold its shape.

The fix is simple: always make a small test swatch before you start.

Here's how:
1. Use your pattern's recommended hook size and yarn weight
2. Chain 20, then work 20 rows in the pattern stitch
3. Measure a 4x4 inch square in the center of your swatch
4. Count the stitches and rows inside that square
5. Compare to the gauge listed in your pattern

If you have MORE stitches than the pattern says → go up a hook size
If you have FEWER stitches → go down a hook size

Simple. And it will save your project every single time.

I actually made a full tutorial on this on the blog — [read it here →]

Happy crocheting,

[Your Name]
```

## After the Sequence: What Happens Next

**If they bought (tag: "buyer"):**
- Remove from sales sequence
- Add to post-purchase sequence (thank-you → usage tips → cross-sell)
- Never send the same sales sequence again for the same product

**If they didn't buy:**
- They stay on your main newsletter list
- Send weekly/bi-weekly value content
- Introduce them to the next product when you launch it
- Run a re-engagement sequence after 60 days of no opens

## How to Avoid the Spam Folder

1. **Use a custom domain email.** Not Gmail. E.g., `hello@yourbrand.com`
2. **Set up SPF, DKIM, DMARC** — Brevo guides you through this
3. **Never use spam words:** FREE!!!!, URGENT, LIMITED TIME, ACT NOW, guaranteed, 100% free
4. **Keep your unsubscribe rate below 0.1%** — if it's higher, your content isn't resonating
5. **Don't send to inactive contacts** — clean your list every 90 days
6. **Start slow:** don't send 10,000 emails on day 1 — warm up your domain gradually
7. **Ask subscribers to whitelist you** — in Email 1: "Add hello@yourbrand.com to your contacts so my emails reach you."
